ProjectStudent Club

UCXR: UCalgary Mixed Reality Club Designing and Manufacturing an Augmented Reality Headset

Nicholas Plowman (president), Toluwanimi Oladiti (mechanical lead), Jack Liu (electrical lead), Adhira John (software lead), Ferzam Mahmood (modelling and research lead), Christian Frisson (advisor)

Abstract

UCXR are designing and manufacturing an augmented reality headset. Elara is their student-designed Augmented Reality headset developed to visualise human anatomy and other complex systems; advancing innovation in medicine, education, engineering design and manufacturing. The ELARA headset is an AR device for real-time 3D visualization and interaction, designed to make technical education more immersive, accessible, and engaging for students and professionals in medicine, engineering, manufacturing, and beyond.

Keywords:  Electrical EngineeringMechanical EngineeringSoftware EngineeringModellingExtended Reality

Waybionic: UCalgary Club Specializing in Robotic Surgery in Space

Lina Dahou (president), Yash Dhillon (electrical), Samipya Rijal (electrical), Yassin Soliman (software), Mujtaba Zia (software), Talha Zafar (software), Abdul Tinwala (mechanical), Zayd Charanek (mechanical), Abdul Karim (biomedical), Ahmeel Pablo (biomedical), Marie Charbonneau (advisor), Christian Frisson (advisor)

Abstract

At WayBionic, we strive to revolutionize remote surgery by developing a bionic arm inspired by the challenges of performing medical procedures in space. Our goal is to create innovative, reliable, and precise tools that empower both astronauts and doctors, whether in orbit or on Earth.
